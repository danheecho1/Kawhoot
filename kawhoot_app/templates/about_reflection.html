Reflections and Notes to Self

       1) I had no idea about responsive designs until recently. I did notice different behaviors at different browser sizes because of display: flex and sizes defined with pixels, but I thought that fitting everything in my viewport would resolve the problem by eliminating the need to scroll. This is embarrassing, but I guess there is first to everything.
       2) I need to work on my JavaScript. I was able to create what I wanted to, but I had almost 100 lines of codes just declaring variables at one point. I still have ugly and repetitive codes that I do not know how to improve.
       3) I had a long break from coding towards the end of this project due to my son being born. Coming back after weeks of time off and getting back to the groove was rough, but I am very happy to mark this project as completed at last.
       4) This project made me realize that I prefer working on the frontend rather than the backend. I thought I had to become a fullstack developer because that's what everyone else is (?), but now I feel okay about focusing on developing my frontend skills.
