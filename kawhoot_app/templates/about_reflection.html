Taking a Quiz

Reflections and Notes to Self

       1) I had a good idea of what features and information I wanted, but it was a headache trying to figure out how to showcase them on the web. The dashboard page is a clear evidence of the struggle.
       2) I have been curious about how to best render search results onto a redirected page (instead of 'return render_template()') after a 'post' route. For Myspice, I ignored the conventional wisdom and used 'return render_template()' to a 'post' route instead of 'return redirect()'. For Kawhoot, I tried passing the search keyword as a variable in the URL. Both seem to work, but I wonder which one is preferable and why.
       3) For the next project: use different frameworks (i.e. Django and React) and/or technologies (i.e. API or ORM), make a responsive or mobile-friendly application (this concept was new to me), and incorporate different types of data formats (i.e. images, videos/animation loops, or sounds).
