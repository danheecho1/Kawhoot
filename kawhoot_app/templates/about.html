About Kawhoot

          
          Kahoot separates the party that creates quizzes from the one that takes quizzes. The latter has no reason to be on Kahoot other than that they are forced to by a teacher, meeting or presentation organizer, trainer, and so on. With Kawhoot, anyone can create quizzes about anything or take any quizzes they want. You want to test your ‘friends’ to see who your real friends are? You want to creep out your crush by taking every single quiz that he or she made? Search quizzes by title, description, or the creator’s username, and take a crack at them all!
          With Kahoot, there is no more competition once your session is over. Who thinks about playing another round of Kahoot? What, you got the perfect score? See if anyone remembers the following day. Kawhoot frames top performers from each quiz and within different categories for all to see. You have to score higher, and you have to take more quizzes. What gives you a reason to come back for more? Where will you be able to win it all?
