About Kawhoot

          Kawhoot is somewhat of a clone of Kahoot (a game-based learning platform) - users can create or take any quiz they want.
          Unlike Kahoot, Kawhoot has a leaderboard for different criteria on the dashboard page as well as one for each quiz. This was Kawhoot's solution to the lack of incentives to come back for more. To earn your position on the leaderboard, you will have to score higher on more quizzes or create more quizzes before others do.
          Kawhoot has the potential to be an extension of SNS platforms (like Myspice) where people can create quizzes about themselves or their interests to spark some friendly competitions. Or you can quiz your 'friends' to see if they really know you. Or take every single quiz that your crush made, and hope that he or she notices you...
