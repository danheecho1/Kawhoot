About Kawhoot

          Kawhoot is somewhat of a clone of Kahoot (a game-based learning platform) - simply put, users can search for, make, or take any quiz they want.
          Unlike Kahoot, Kawhoot has a leaderboard for different criteria on the dashboard page as well as one for each quiz. This was Kawhoot's solution to the lack of incentives to come back for more. To earn your position on the leaderboard, you will have to score higher on more quizzes as well as create more quizzes.
          Kawhoot has the potential to be an extension of SNS platforms (like Myspice) where people can create quizzes about themselves or their interests to bring about some friendly competitions. Or you can see which 'friends' really know you. Or take every single quiz that someone cute wrote, and hope that he or she notices you...
